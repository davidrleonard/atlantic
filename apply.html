This is Daniel. His story is why we help.

Daniel's water was turned off for 6 months. His fixed income made it impossible to pay back bills. Daniel traveled to a neighborhood center for showers and washed his dishes in a neighbor's backyard.

The Human Utility paid $1,400 in back payments to help Daniel catch up.
